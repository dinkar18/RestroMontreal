Seoul Chako is a restaurant that offers an authentic formula of real Korean grills. During your visit, you will find a friendly atmosphere and a new concept of cuisine in an absolutely great decor! A typical Korean grill is integrated into each table and allows you to prepare your own grill! This principle makes it an ideal place to organize a family barbecue or with friends, and gives you the opportunity to concoct a unique meal, in good company!
